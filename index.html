BONES

To win be the first player to reach 10,000 points.

  • 1.To start, each player rolls all the dice. The player with the highest roll goes first.
  • 2.To get points on the board your first roll must be a minimum of 600 points.
  • 3.On your turn, you roll all five dice. You can keep rolling as long as you roll at least one 1, 5, or three-of-a-kind. If you roll a zero on your first roll, your turn is over and you lose all points from your previous roll.
  • 4.You can score points for:
    • Ones:100 points for each 1 rolled
    • Fives:50 points for each 5 rolled
    • Three of a kind:Three of a kind: 1000 points for three dice of the same number
    • Four of a kind:2000 points for four dice of the same number
    • Yahtzee:10,000 points for all five dice of the same number
    • Full house: 200 points for three of one number and two of another number
    • Small straight:300 points for 1-2-3-4-5
    • Large straight400 points for 2-3-4-5-6
  • 5.If you end your turn without scoring any points, you lose all points from your previous roll.
  • 6.The first player to reach 10,000 points wins.
